Robotic Surgery

    Robotic Surgery

    Robotic Surgery is a branch of medical science that uses a robotic
    arm controlled with acomputer by a surgeon. Small tools attached
    to the robotic arm are then used to perform the surgery by copying
    the hand movements of the surgeon as he controls the robotic
    arm. With this method being more convenient for the surgeon due
    to a perfect view of the area under surgery, Apollo’s Robotic
    Surgery Department is equipped with a skilled team of the best
    Surgeons. Apollo Hospitals is your one-stop destination to book
    the finest Robot-assisted Surgeons in your town
